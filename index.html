About This Project

Mitrovica is a city settlement and seat of the municipality of the same name, located in the northern part of Kosovo and Metohija. It is divided into the northern and southern parts. Here, we want to provide information related to documents, in which way, we can get some information for obtaining them. This approach is open to all citizens, no matter where they live.

What caused us to create such a site is the fact that we are very difficult to get to our documents precisely because of the lack of information from the competent authorities where we should go, and what to look for.

This idea was supported by girls from Girls Coding Kosova, so we are pleased to present you and we hope that it will make it easier for You in the future to access all the documents and information that are relevant to Your important.
